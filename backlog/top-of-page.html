Top of page pop-up

Top of page adds/replaces current top of page link at bottom of mobile only resolution.

It only pops up when user scrolls up on a page to minimize interuption of the pop-up and with added assumption the user may scroll up to try and get to the top of the page.

It shows up on lower right for desktop and bottom center for mobile (to minimize accidental press by thumbs).
